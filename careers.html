ONÉSIMUS DEFENSE IS CURRENTLY SEEKING RESUMES FOR PROFESSIONALS TO FULFILL INTERMITTENT INSTRUCTOR OPPORTUNITIES WITHIN AND OUTSIDE THE CONTINENTAL US.

  • Fall Protection
  • Industrial Rescue
  • Rope Rescue
  • High Angle Rescue
  • Tower Rescue
  • SPRAT/IRATA/ProBoard Certifications
  • Confined Space Rescue (to include Maritime/Marine Chemist)
  • Certified Industrial Hygienist
  • Fire and Life Safety
  • Various OSHA workplace safety programs
  • First Responder
