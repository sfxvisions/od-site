Safety Services

Overview

Onésimus Defense’s Safety Services Division provides clients with world-class expertise in training, equipping, and knowledge-management in the most current and up to date standards as promulgated by federal and
industrial safety oversight agencies, such as OSHA, ANSI, ASSE, and the NFPA.  Our professionals integrate with these governing agencies to stay abreast of evolving standards and best practices to provide our clients with the best solution to their training, first responder, rescue and disaster preparedness needs. From the Department of Defense, to multiple other federal agencies, to local municipalities, to the private sector, Onésimus Defense provides our partners with a total solution to their training, equipping and readiness challenges.
