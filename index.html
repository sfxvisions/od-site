Who we are...

Onésimus Defense was formed when two industry professionals, both of whom held executive leadership positions within a small-company attempting to transition to large-company, recognized our industry’s need for a “back-to-basics” approach to customer service. This approach focuses on lessons learned, gained by applying specifically dedicated efforts to ensure the customer is integrally involved in each and every step of the process, thereby developing the best solution—the first time. This means that the members of Onésimus Defense’s executive leadership team not only recognize, but have internalized this principal commitment that the customer will never have to wait. 

Onésimus Defense set out to constantly serve its customers, clients and partners with exceptionally beneficial service – and to always, without exception, be viewed by those partners as necessary and relevant.
